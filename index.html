Make Your Site Move

VideoSplash is a CSS library for HTML5 video backgrounds and a library of free video files. VideoSplash is designed to work with Twitter Bootstrap.

What is VideoSplash?

Free Video Files

A growing library of free (no restrictions) video files for use in your HTML5 projects. Videos are shot with a GoPro Hero3 Black at 1080p/60fps and have had the audio track removed to reduce file size.

CSS for Video Backgrounds

Use the video_splash.css file to quickly setup video backgrounds in your HTML5 pages. Perfect for home pages, landing pages, and more. VideoSplash CSS is designed to work with Twitter Bootstrap.
